Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026 · Last updated: May 21, 2026

Nova Concierge ("Nova," "we," "us," or "our") operates a concierge provisioning service based in California. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services. We are committed to handling your data responsibly and in compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and other applicable laws.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Fulfill orders: Process, coordinate, and deliver your concierge order
  • Communicate with you: Send quotes, order updates, substitution questions, delivery confirmations, and follow-ups via SMS and phone
  • Respond to inquiries: Reply to messages submitted through our contact form
  • Improve our services: Analyze usage patterns to enhance the website and ordering experience
  • Legal compliance: Meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our Terms of Service

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information for unrelated marketing purposes.

3. SMS & Phone Communications

When you submit an order, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages and phone calls from Nova at the number you provide. This consent is governed by the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), 47 U.S.C. § 227.

  • Types of messages: Quotes, payment links, order updates, substitution inquiries, delivery confirmations, and post-delivery follow-ups
  • Frequency: Varies per order, typically 3–10 messages per order cycle
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to opt out of non-transactional messages. Note: opting out of transactional messages during an active order may prevent us from fulfilling your request
  • Cost: Standard message and data rates from your carrier may apply

Your consent to receive SMS is not a condition of purchasing our services, except as reasonably necessary to coordinate your specific order.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with the following parties, and only to the extent necessary:

  • Property managers/partners: Your name, check-in details, and order timing (not your full order) may be shared with the property manager to coordinate access
  • Third-party alcohol providers: If your order includes alcohol, your name, delivery address, and alcohol-specific items are shared with a licensed third-party fulfillment provider
  • Payment processors: Your payment information is handled by our third-party payment processor. We never see or store your full card number
  • Service providers: We may use third-party tools for analytics, hosting, and SMS delivery that process data on our behalf under contractual obligations
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect our rights, safety, or property

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights Under California Law

If you are a California resident, the CCPA and CPRA grant you the following rights:

  • Right to know: You may request a copy of the personal information we have collected about you, including the categories and sources of data, the business purpose for collecting it, and the categories of third parties with whom it was shared. Under CPRA, this right extends to data collected beyond the preceding 12 months where feasible
  • Right to delete: You may request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain legal exceptions (e.g., legal compliance, completing a transaction)
  • Right to correct: You may request that we correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information: You may direct us to limit the use of sensitive personal information (such as allergy data) to purposes necessary to provide our services
  • Right to opt out of sale/sharing: We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. There is nothing to opt out of
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request through our contact form or by mail to: Nova Concierge, Newport Beach, CA. You may also designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf; we will require verification of the agent's authority. We will verify your identity before processing your request and respond within 45 days as required by law.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., form state, session persistence)
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. If we use specific third-party analytics (e.g., Cloudflare Web Analytics), we will link to their privacy policy here. No personal data is sold to advertisers

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or cross-site tracking. Your browser's "Do Not Track" signal and Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal are respected.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

  • Order data: Retained for 3 years after your last order for service quality, dispute resolution, tax obligations, and legal compliance
  • Contact form submissions: Retained for 1 year
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized within 90 days

You may request deletion at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

8. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and secure third-party providers.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will promptly delete it. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Notice at Collection

At or before the point of collection, we provide notice of the categories of personal information being collected and the purposes for which it will be used. On our order form, this notice is provided through the consent checkbox and a link to this Privacy Policy. On our contact form, this notice is provided through the form itself and the linked policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. If we make material changes to how we handle your personal information, we will provide notice through the website or direct communication. Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Users Outside California

Our services are primarily offered in California. If you access our website or use our services from outside California, your information will be processed in the United States. By using our services, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States, which may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.
